hi! my name's cammie (she/her) and i'm a 27 year old trans musician, dj, creative technologist, hobbyist game developer, and experience designer currently based in chicago, il. some may know me from my years in the theme park industry working on some really really cool stuff. others may know me for my horrible takes and shitposts on twitter. wherever you come from, welcome! when i'm not working on freelance work and various other creative endeavors, i also help run limiting factor records with my friend ellie/kinoteki/burialposter (check out their music, it's really good!). some of my hobbies as of late include traveling, working through my games backlog, meowing, spinning in circles, raving, and hanging out with my friends.

i created this site as a way to coalesce the various different parts of my life into one easily digestible, low calorie package! * i also wanted to create a space to unplug from social media and share my more long-form musings with anyone who cares to listen, so if you've read this far, thank you! :3 i would not be where i am today without the support of all of the friends and family i've made over the years in every corner of the internet. i love you all! <3

feel free to reach me on discord, signal, or email me!

* note: please don't eat my website. i worked very hard on it and if it's anything like my own stomach it will give you horrible, horrible indigestion. i can also make no guarantees as to any sort of nutritional value.
